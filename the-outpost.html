The Outpost

Prison of Infinite Dreams

About the Project

The Outpost is an experimental short film created in Unreal Engine using MetaHumans and full-body motion capture. It explores identity, time, and the nature of consciousness — blending cinematic storytelling with real-time technology to push the boundaries of digital filmmaking.

Work in Progress — This project is currently in development as part of an Unreal Engine MegaGrant application.
