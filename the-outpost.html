Elevator Pitch

A cinematic science-fiction short film made in Unreal Engine with MetaHumans. A man and his robot companion awaken to the truth: time is a cage, memory a trap, and imagination a threat to the gods who built their prison.

Project Overview

The Outpost explores consciousness, control, and the limits of reality. Stranded at a remote outpost on Kepler-16b, Alex—a hyper-real MetaHuman—and his robot Rex begin remembering prior loops. As their world “stutters” and resets, they uncover a design: reality as an elegant, self-repairing prison built to contain the potential of human imagination.

Why Unreal Engine

  • MetaHumans for facial nuance and grounded performance
  • Performance capture (body + Live Link face)
  • Virtual cinematography & Sequencer for in-engine shot design
  • Real-time “reality stutters” rendered live as narrative events
