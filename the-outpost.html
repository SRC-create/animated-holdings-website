Project Overview

The Outpost is a philosophical science-fiction short exploring consciousness, control, and the limits of reality. Stranded at a remote outpost on Kepler-16b, Alex—a hyper-real MetaHuman—and his robot Rex begin remembering prior loops. As their world “stutters” and resets, they uncover a design: reality as an elegant, self-repairing prison built to contain the power of human imagination.

Why Unreal Engine

  • MetaHumans for facial nuance and grounded performance
  • Body + Live Link face for unified performance capture
  • Sequencer & virtual cinematography for in-engine shot design
  • Real-time “reality stutters”—environment fractures rendered live in UE
